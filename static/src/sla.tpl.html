Commercial use of Unpaywall data

The Unpaywall dataset is free for noncommercial use. However, for commercial use you'll need to sign up for the Service-Level Agreement (SLA). This lets you:

  • Use the downloaded dataset for commercial purposes.
  • Get weekly updates on what changed in the Unpaywall database. So, if you start with the download file, then subscribe to the weekly updates, you'll be able to maintain an exact copy of the Unpaywall database, on your own local servers.
  • Get enterprise-level service and support established and guaranteed in writing.
  • Help support Unpaywall and keep it sustainable over the long term.

Of course, if you don't need these features, you can always just use the API; it's free to everyone for any use.

Our pricing on the SLA is flexible, depending on your revenue and use-case. So if you're interested, please contact us; we'd love to hear from you!
